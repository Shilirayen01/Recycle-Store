Recycling Tips

  • Start recycling your plastic waste.
  • Separate recyclables from non-recyclables.
  • Donate clothes and electronics instead of throwing them away.
  • Compost organic waste.

Our Mission

Our goal is to raise awareness about the importance of recycling and help communities adopt eco-friendly practices.

Wood recycling promotes sustainable practices by reusing discarded wood materials create new products or for energy production. This helps reduce deforestation, minimize landfill waste, and conserve valuable natural resources.

Glass recycling is an essential practice that helps conserve natural resources and reduce energy consumption. By reprocessing used glass bottles and jars, we minimize waste in landfills and contribute to a more sustainable environment.

Plastic waste harms the environment and marine life, taking centuries to decompose. By reusing and recycling, we can reduce its impact and protect our oceans for future generations.
